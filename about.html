Lead Physiotherapist

Dr. Radhe Patel

BPT, MPT (Orthopedics) | Certified Manual Therapist

With over a decade of clinical excellence, Dr. Radhe Patel is the founder and lead physiotherapist at Neurofit Advance Physiotherapy Center. Known for a patient-first approach, Dr. Patel combines advanced manual therapy techniques with cutting-edge rehabilitation technology to deliver exceptional outcomes.

Specializing in orthopedic and neurological physiotherapy, Dr. Patel has successfully treated over 5,000 patients, helping them regain mobility, overcome pain, and return to their active lifestyles. His expertise spans from complex post-surgical rehabilitation to sports injury management and neurological recovery.

"My philosophy is simple — every patient deserves personalized, evidence-based care that addresses the root cause of their condition, not just the symptoms."
