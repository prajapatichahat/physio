Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Online Guidance

How does online physiotherapy guidance work?

After booking, you'll receive a video call link. During the session, our physiotherapist will assess your condition through video, demonstrate exercises, and provide a personalized treatment plan. You'll receive a digital prescription and follow-up support.

Is online consultation as effective as in-person visits?

For initial assessment, exercise guidance, and follow-ups, online consultation is very effective. However, for hands-on treatments like manual therapy or electrotherapy, in-person visits are recommended. Our doctor will advise you accordingly.

What platform do you use for video calls?

We primarily use WhatsApp Video Call or Google Meet for consultations. The link will be shared with you before your scheduled appointment time.

How much does online guidance cost?

Online consultation fees are very affordable and lower than in-clinic visits. Contact us for current pricing. Follow-up consultations are available at discounted rates.
