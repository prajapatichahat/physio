What is Back Pain Treatment?

Back pain is one of the most common conditions affecting people of all ages. At Neurofit Advance Physiotherapy Center, we use a combination of manual therapy, electrotherapy, and therapeutic exercises to provide lasting relief from both acute and chronic back pain.

Our evidence-based approach targets the root cause of your pain, not just the symptoms. Whether you're dealing with a herniated disc, sciatica, muscle strain, or postural issues, our specialized treatment protocols are designed to restore your mobility and get you back to your active life.

Our Treatment Process

Step 1: Detailed Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of your condition, posture, range of motion, and pain patterns to create a personalized treatment plan.

Step 2: Pain Management

Immediate pain relief through advanced modalities including ultrasound therapy, TENS, IFT, and manual techniques.

Step 3: Therapeutic Exercises

Customized exercise program to strengthen core muscles, improve flexibility, and prevent recurrence.

Step 4: Posture Correction

Ergonomic advice and postural training to maintain proper alignment in daily activities and work.

Step 5: Maintenance & Prevention

Home exercise program and regular follow-ups to ensure long-term recovery and prevent future episodes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many sessions will I need?

Most patients experience significant relief within 8-15 sessions. The exact number depends on the severity and chronicity of your condition. Our doctor will provide a clear treatment plan after your initial assessment.

Is physiotherapy painful?

Physiotherapy should not cause additional pain. While some techniques may cause mild discomfort during treatment, the overall goal is pain relief. Our therapists always work within your comfort level.

Can I continue my daily activities during treatment?

Yes, in most cases you can continue your daily activities. Our therapist will advise you on any modifications needed and provide ergonomic tips for your workplace and home.

Do you offer home visits for treatment?

Yes, we offer home physiotherapy visits for patients who are unable to travel to the clinic. Contact us to schedule a home visit appointment.
